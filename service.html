Servicing at Slaven Mazda

Slaven Mazda’s service department has an experienced team of factory trained technicians with the latest in Mazda electronic and diagnostic equipment to allow us to ensure your Mazda is running at its peak in performance and economy and, most importantly, safety.

We operate from Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM to 5:30 PM, and have the facilities to service over 40 vehicles a day. We have a range of facilities available to clients who leave their Mazdas with us. If you are happy to wait, we offer WI-FI, complimentary coffee and a kid’s play corner near our lounge area. If you need to get about your business or head to work, we offer a bus run and complimentary loan cars.

We complete all log book services as part of Mazda’s Service Select Program, the pricing of which is listed on Mazda Australia’s website and we only use Mazda genuine spare parts.
