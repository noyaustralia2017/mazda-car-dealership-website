Welcome to Slaven Mazda Canberra

Slaven Mazda has an impressive past and an exciting future. Located at 140 Melrose Drive, Phillip in the ACT, Slaven Mazda has almost 40 years’ experience in the motor vehicle industry. We provide the following services:

New Car Sales

Servicing

Genuine Parts & Accesories

Finance & Insurance

From the introduction of such legendary vehicles as the RX-7, MX-5 and 323, Mazda continues to lead the way with the exciting range of Zoom Zoom vehicles currently available.

At Slaven Mazda we pride ourselves on the quality of our products and our service. With over 40 staff across the dealership, our focus is on customer satisfaction. Whether it be through the purchase of your new Mazda, or in the care of it for years to come, you’ll always receive our highest quality of service.
