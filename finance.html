Finance & Insurance at Slaven Mazda

When you choose Slaven Mazda, we’ll make certain that you have a smooth, straightforward and efficient experience. We offer our customers competitive personal and business finance options for the purchase of your vehicle.

Our Business Manager, Shayne Brilley will arrange finance services to approved customers on the same day. You may speak with him directly to schedule your appointment by calling (02) 6208 4421.

Some of the types of Finance we offer are:

Consumer Finance

This finance option is for the buyer who predominately drives their vehicle for private use.

Commercial Hire Purchase

This is the perfect finance option for strictly business usage.

Chattel Mortgage

Designed for individual businesses that trade on a cash basis to claim back GST when they lodge their next business Activity Statement (BAS.)

Finance Lease

This tax effective tool will assist in meeting your needs. This lease allows your business to maintain and conserve your business cash flow and use hard earned capital more effectively.

Novated Lease

This lease allows for a portion of your salary to be deducted each month in order to pay for your new vehicle. It’s an affordable way to get the new Mazda you’ve always wanted.




At Slaven Mazda, we partner with St George Bank to offer you competitive and flexible interest rates. Our current interest rate through St George is 1% for the EOFY Sale. Use our online calculator to calculate your repayments.

Your new Mazda is an investment and we want to help you to protect it as such. We offer a variety of insurance and warranty options to help protect your new vehicle from Slaven Mazda:

Comprehensive Motor Insurance

Income Protection

GAP Insurance

Extended Warranty
